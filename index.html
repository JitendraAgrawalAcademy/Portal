About Our Academy

The core purpose of Jitendra Agrawal Academy is to transform complex academic problems into interactive, game-based learning experiences for children, making education engaging, intuitive, and fun.

The academy was founded by Jitendra Agrawal, a versatile visionary who is a Computer Engineer, Advocate, and Author. He is currently practicing law at the District and Sessions Court, Jalaun at Orai.
